Taking the online video course
Developers can learn how to build a Roku app from the ground up through the SceneGraph: Build an app online video course . This course features a set of 13 video lessons and a comprehensive sample app that walks developers through each step in building a basic, high-performance Roku app that passes certification. This course is ideal for developers that are new to the Roku platform and want a simple, streamlined path for quickly understanding Roku development.
The SceneGraph: Build an app online video course is available on YouTube , the Roku developers video portal , and the Roku Developers app on your Roku device.
